# A number is prime or not?

n=int(input('Enter a number:'))
c=0
for i in range(1,n+1):
    if (n%i==0):
        c=c+1
if (c==2):
    print('The number',n,'is Prime.')
else:
    print('The number',n,'is NOT Prime.')

Output:-
Enter a number:59
The number 59 is Prime.
